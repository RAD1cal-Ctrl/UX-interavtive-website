Buttermilk Pancakes with Sticky Bananas and Brazilian Nuts

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped Brazilian nuts
  • Maple syrup, for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the buttermilk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Be careful not to overmix; a few lumps are okay. Let the batter rest for about 10 minutes.
  4. While the batter is resting, prepare the sticky bananas. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add the sliced bananas, brown sugar, and chopped Brazilian nuts. Cook for 2-3 minutes, stirring gently, until the bananas are caramelized and the nuts are toasted. Remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Heat a non-stick griddle or skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease the surface with butter or cooking spray.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of pancake batter onto the griddle for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Transfer the cooked pancakes to a serving plate and keep warm. Repeat the process with the remaining batter, adding more butter or cooking spray to the griddle as needed.
  8. Serve the buttermilk pancakes topped with the sticky bananas and Brazilian nuts. Drizzle with maple syrup.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Buttermilk Pancakes with Sticky Bananas and Brazilian Nuts!
